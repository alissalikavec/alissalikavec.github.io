A bit about me..

I love design and development, and loooove writing clean, reusable code. JavaScript is my bread and butter! As a designer, I utilize a thorough design process that includes user research, testing and iterating designs based on user feedback, followed by putting together a series of deliverables and/or coding out the final product. Wireframes? Yes. User flows and site maps? OH yeah. Code and brand style guides and up-to-date documentation on a project’s architecture? You’d better believe it!

Organizing and documenting the design and development processes for my team and clients is a passion of mine. Designing for accessibility is important to me too! I believe that users should be advocated for whenever possible, and I love building products that users appreciate. I enjoy meeting with clients to discuss their needs - there’s nothing better than hearing a user say “that was so easy!” or “I wish all sites were like this!”

I believe that good UX starts with a relationship between designers and developers, and will never come from a designer being brought in to add colors and a shiny logo.

Aside from that, I love nature, my pug Murphy and cat Maya, and being on the water! Feel free to drop a line to introduce yourself.
